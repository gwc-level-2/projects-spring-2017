Impact Project Mission Statement

This website and game were created as part of our 2017 Spring "Girls Who Code" Impact Project. We wish to help illustrate the issue of access to fresh produce in food deserts. A food desert, as defined by the CDC is "areas that lack access to affordable fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lowfat milk, and other foods that make up the full range of a healthy diet."1

The Girls Who Code Level 2 class at the University of Wisconsin - Milwaukee created the game and collection of resources for these purposes: to spread awareness of food deserts, to build an understanding of the challenges of living in a food desert, and to show citizens across the country how they can help.
