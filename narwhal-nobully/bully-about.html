About Us

We are 5 girls in Girls Who Code Level 2 at the University of Wisconsin - Milwaukee. Our final Impact Project was to create a website to help people. We chose bullying as our topic because it impacts many kids. Our website has a game that people can play to understand a bullying. We had many challenges creating the website coding and coding the game, but we worked together and got it done.

Why We Chose Narwhals

We chose narwhals because we think that they are cute and amazing creatures. They are special unicorns of the sea. Our main character is a narwhal named Narwhal Nobully. He teaches kids how to respond to bullying. While creating the narwhal game, we had a couple of hiccups, but in the end we solved our problem.

You Become the Bystander

One of the reasons we decided to make the player of our game the bystander is that many people have been a bystander to bullying and before and didn't know what to do. Second, it made more sense in our game, since our hero is Narwhal Nobully. By seeing what Narwhal does, the bystander will also learn what to do.
